Getting Started

Daps is a demand-first marketplace that connects fans with professional and amateur athletes. Through our platform, fans can submit "bounties" — custom requests for unique collectibles (like game-worn jerseys or autographed items) or personalized experiences (such as video calls, social media shoutouts, or in-person meetups) directly to their favorite athletes.

Unlike traditional marketplaces where athletes list items they're willing to sell, Daps flips the model: you tell us what you want, and athletes decide whether to accept your offer, deny it, or send you a counteroffer.

Getting started is easy:

  • Create a free account by signing up with your email
  • Browse athletes on our platform or search for your favorite sports figure
  • Submit a bounty by describing what you want and your offer price
  • Wait for the athlete to respond (within 14 days)

You won't be charged anything until an athlete accepts your bounty.

Creating an account and browsing athletes is completely free. You only pay when an athlete accepts your bounty. Our platform charges a service fee on each transaction to keep Daps running, but 85% of the revenue goes directly to the athlete.

There are no subscription fees or hidden charges — you only pay for the experiences and collectibles you receive.

Submitting Bounties

You can request almost anything from athletes, including:

Collectibles:

  • Game-worn jerseys, shoes, or equipment
  • Autographed items (photos, memorabilia, trading cards)
  • Personal items from the athlete

Digital Experiences:

  • Video calls or FaceTime sessions
  • Personalized video messages
  • Social media shoutouts or follows
  • Help with fantasy sports or March Madness brackets
  • Virtual tours (like a sneaker closet tour)

In-Person Experiences:

  • Meet-and-greets before or after games
  • Attend a practice or training session
  • Play pickup basketball or workout together
  • Dinner or coffee meetups

If you're unsure about pricing, our platform will provide a price recommendation based on similar bounties and the athlete's typical rates. You can accept this recommendation or adjust your offer based on your budget.

Keep in mind that athletes are more likely to accept competitive offers. If your offer is too low, the athlete may send you a counteroffer with their preferred price.

You can submit a bounty request to any athlete or sports figure, even if they're not currently on our platform. We'll do our best to reach out to them on your behalf.

However, athletes who are already registered on Daps are more likely to respond quickly since they actively monitor their bounty requests.

Athletes have up to 14 days to respond to your bounty. You'll receive a notification once the athlete accepts, denies, or counteroffers your request.

Many athletes respond much faster than 14 days, but we allow this window to account for their busy schedules during the season.

Payment & Transactions

You will only be charged if and when an athlete accepts your bounty. When you submit a bounty, we'll securely store your payment information, but no charges will be made until acceptance.

If the athlete denies your request or doesn't respond within 14 days, you won't be charged anything.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. All payments are processed securely through our third-party payment processor.

Our service fee is included in the transaction and automatically deducted before payment is sent to the athlete. There are no hidden fees, but you may be responsible for applicable taxes depending on your location.

Athletes receive 85% of the total payment, with 15% going to Daps to maintain and improve the platform.

All payments are generally non-refundable once an athlete has accepted your bounty and begun fulfilling it. However, if a collectible is not delivered or an experience is not completed within a reasonable timeframe, we may process a refund.

If you have concerns about your bounty fulfillment, please contact our support team at hey@getdaps.io and we'll work to resolve the issue.

If an athlete believes your offer is too low or wants to negotiate terms, they can send you a counteroffer. You'll receive a notification with their proposed price or modified terms.

You can then choose to accept the counteroffer, decline it, or submit a new bounty with adjusted terms. No charges will be made until you accept a counteroffer.

Fulfillment & Delivery

Once an athlete accepts your bounty, you'll receive detailed instructions on fulfillment:

  • Physical collectibles will be shipped to the address you provided. You'll receive tracking information once the item ships.
  • Digital experiences (like video calls or messages) will be coordinated directly between you and the athlete or through our platform.
  • In-person experiences require mutual coordination on timing and location. We'll facilitate this communication.

If your collectible arrives damaged or not as described, please contact us immediately at hey@getdaps.io with photos of the damage. We'll work with you and the athlete to resolve the issue, which may include a replacement, partial refund, or full refund depending on the circumstances.

Delivery times vary depending on the type of bounty:

  • Physical collectibles: Typically ship within 7-14 days after acceptance
  • Digital experiences: Can be scheduled within a few days to a few weeks based on athlete availability
  • In-person experiences: Require more coordination and may take several weeks to arrange

Athletes are encouraged to fulfill bounties promptly, but their schedules (especially during the season) may affect timing.
